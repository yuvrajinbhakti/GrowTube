Virality — How to Make Videos Blow Up
Viral nahi hona lucky nahi hota — it's a formula

Every video that blows up has specific ingredients. You can engineer these on purpose. Here's exactly what works in the education niche on YouTube India.

⚡ The 5 Virality Triggers
1. 🎯 Emotion in the Thumbnail

Shock, curiosity, or urgency makes people click. Instead of a plain face, show a surprised or "wait what?" expression with text like "यह कोई नहीं बताता 👀" or "Result आ गया 😱"

2. 🪝 The First 30 Seconds Hook

If viewers don't stay past 30 seconds, YouTube stops recommending the video. Start with: "Aaj main tumhe woh cheez bataunga jo 90% students nahi jaante…" — never start with "Namaste, aaj ke video mein hum dekhenge..."

3. 📣 Shareability

Videos get shared when they're relatable or solve an urgent problem. "B.Com mein fail hone ke 5 reasons" will be shared in every B.Com WhatsApp group in PU. Think: "Would I share this in my class group?"

4. 📅 Timing

Post exam prep videos 3 weeks before exams, result videos the day results are expected, and datesheet videos the day it's announced. Timing = 10x the views for the same video.

5. 💬 Comment Bait (Ethical)

End every video with a question: "Comment mein batao — tumhara sabse mushkil subject kaun sa hai?" Comments signal the algorithm that your video is engaging. More comments = more reach.
